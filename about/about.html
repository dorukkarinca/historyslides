About

My initial motive behind writing this application was to assist history teachers in teaching WWI. But it can be scaled up to include world maps of different timelines. The maps are currently limited to European boundaries because of performance limitations.

Please note that this app is optimized for Google Chrome, and Internet Explorer is not supported.
